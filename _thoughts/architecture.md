Clean architecture, hexagonal, etc architectures are blueprints.

When you "architect" using those blueprints, you are not designing architecture for the software, you are filling in blanks in the blueprint with your names.

Which is fine if you want everything to look the same.

Architecting is looking at the problem and finding the most appropriate solution for the task.

When I build a house, I could take one of existing plans and stretch or shrink it, change the colors. Instead, I know the size of my family, our traditions and habits. I design a house that fits us best.
